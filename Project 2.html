Introduction¶

In this project we will analysis movies data.

in particular, we will be intrested in Budget, Revenue, Vote Average, Release Year and Release Date, we have 4 research question that we will discusses during this project,

we choose the independent attribute's as:

  • Vote Average
  • Release Year
  • Release Date
  • Revenue

we choose the dependent attribute's as:

  • Budget

Research Questions:¶

  • Does spending more Budget increase the chance of getting high Vote Average ?
  • Does the cost of producing a movie is getting higher over the years ?
  • Does the producing of movie is getting higher over the years ?
  • Does the summer movies gets more revenues than the other seasons movies in average ?

Questions Answers:¶

Question 1/ Does spending more Budget increase the chance of getting high Vote Average ?¶

the plot looks little a bit skewed left bell, so the answer is: Yes, the chance of getting higher vote average has increaced.

Question 2/ Does the cost of producing a movie is getting higher over the years ?¶

the plot looks skewed left, so the answer is: Yes, the cost of producing a movie is getting higher, but the last few years has droped a little bit.

Question 3/ Does the producing of movie is getting higher over the years ?¶

the plot looks skewed left perfectly, so the answer is: Yes, the producing of movie is getting higher over the years.

Question 4/ Does the summer movies gets more revenues than the other seasons movies in average ?¶

the bars shows that the average of the all seasons movies is higher than the summer movies, so the answer is: No, the summer movies doesn't get more revenues than the other seasons movies in average.
